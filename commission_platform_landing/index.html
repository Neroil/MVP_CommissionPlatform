Why a New Commissioning Platform?

This platform offers a new and intuitive way to commission and follow your favorite artists.
As both a developer and an artist, I created this project to help artists and their fans
connect and collaborate more efficiently.

Platform Features

Discover how our platform makes the commissioning process easier

  • Get Notified About Open Slots

    Stop checking Twitter daily. Get automatic notifications when your favorite artists open commissions.

  • Find Artists By Style

    Search for artists using our global tagging system based on style, medium, and content, favourite topics, and more.

  • Artist Reviews and Time to Completion

    Clients can rate their experience with artists and indicate how long they had to wait.

  • Track Your Commission Status

    Get real-time updates on your commission status, including progress and estimated completion time. See also your spot in an artist's queue.

  • Manage Your Schedule

    Easily open and close commissions, set availability dates, and organize your queue all in one place.

  • Analytics Dashboard

    Track your commission history, income, and client demographics to grow your art business.
